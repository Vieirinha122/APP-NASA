About

Our goal is to provide an interactive and accessible platform that empowers citizens and policymakers to understand and visualize greenhouse gas emissions in their communities. By using satellite data and advanced models, we aim to raise awareness about the impact of human and natural activities on climate change, helping to create effective solutions for a more sustainable future.

Greenhouse gases like carbon dioxide (CO₂), methane (CH₄), and nitrogen oxides (NO₂) come from both natural and human-made sources. Natural sources include volcanoes, oceans, and respiration of living organisms. However, human activities such as transportation, agriculture, and fossil fuel burning have significantly increased these emissions. Natural sinks, like forests and oceans, help absorb these gases, but they are struggling to keep up with the growing anthropogenic emissions.
